## 3. 去 AI 味规则

### 3.1 保留

- 事实先行
- 直接
- 克制
- 给具体数字

### 3.2 删除

- “I am excited to share...”
- “This groundbreaking framework...”
- “This work will revolutionize...”
- “I would be deeply honored...”
- “I look forward to hearing your valuable thoughts...”

### 3.3 替换

把：

> I am excited to share my groundbreaking work...

改成：

> I am writing to share a draft that may be relevant to your work on...

把：

> We believe this result is of broad and fundamental importance...

改成：

> The result may be relevant to current discussions on...
