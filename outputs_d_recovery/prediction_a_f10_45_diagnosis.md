## 4. Summary

The 4D<5D weakness at N=20 is driven by:
1. ΔlogH(5D−4D) ≈ +2.6 at N=20 (5D has more entropy)
2. d_eff(5D)≈4.4 is close to d*=4.1 → small penalty gap (0.09−0.01=0.08 per element)
3. At N=20, total gap = 0.08×20 = 1.6 < 2.6 (logH dominates)

Asymmetric well with γ_hi > γ_lo can partially fix this.
