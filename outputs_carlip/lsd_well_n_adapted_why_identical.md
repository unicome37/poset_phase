## A4: Robustness Check

Max c* variation across modes: **0.0993**
Max w* variation across modes: **0.1012**
Min Lor4D margin (F_runner-up - F_Lor4D): **0.0428**

**Sensitivity test**: For Lor4D to lose #1 at N=16 (margin ≈ 0.0428), width center would need to shift by ~0.0925 from Lor4D's value.


## Conclusion

Three modes give identical rankings because:

1. **d_eff term dominates discrimination** between categories (Lor vs Layered vs KR) — this term uses fixed d*=4, no mode dependence

2. **width term is the strongest within-category separator** (γ=5.0) but Lor4D-to-runner-up margin in width is large

3. **c*(N)/w*(N) variations are O(0.01-0.05)** while minimum margin is O(0.043) → variations are negligible vs margin

4. The well potential F ∝ (x-x*)² is QUADRATIC — small shifts in x* near the minimum produce O(Δx*²) ≈ 10⁻⁴ changes, which cannot flip any ranking
