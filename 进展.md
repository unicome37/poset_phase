## 3.3 当前可安全结论（建议写法）

**推荐口径**：

> Curvature robustness is background-dependent. In the current tested window, de Sitter and weak-field Schwarzschild remain compatible with the local-basin picture, whereas FLRW at κ=1.0 exhibits boundary-sensitive top-2 instability (lowN hard-fail signal, highN partial recovery without hard-fail trigger).

避免口径：

- “weak/mild curvature uniformly robust”（统一稳健）

**可直接引用的最终段落**：

> In the completed metric-faithful PhaseA run (P0), FLRW at $\kappa=1.0$ shows boundary-sensitive degradation in the metric branch, whereas the proxy branch remains top-2 throughout. Across 10/10 seeds (60 cells), the metric branch records a fail ratio of $11/60=0.183$, while the proxy branch stays at $0/60$. All $\kappa=0.3$ cells remain top-2, and the observed degradation at $\kappa=1.0$ appears as dispersed boundary-sensitive events rather than a concentrated collapse. Accordingly, the metric-faithful PhaseA result supports a background-dependent robustness reading and does not trigger the pre-registered hard-fail criterion.
